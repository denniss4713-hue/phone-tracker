# Privacy Policy for Phone GPS Tracker **Last Updated:** January 2025 ## 1. Introduction Phone GPS Tracker is a mobile application that allows users to track the location of devices and perform phone number lookups. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data. ## 2. Information We Collect ### Location Data - **GPS Location:** When you choose to share your location, the app accesses your device's GPS to obtain real-time location coordinates. - **Location Sharing:** Users can voluntarily share their location with other devices they choose. - **Phone Number Lookup:** The app performs lookups based on country codes to show approximate location. This does NOT provide real-time GPS tracking of phone numbers. ### Device Information - Device name and phone number (if provided by the user) - Unique device identifiers (generated locally) ### Data Storage - All data is stored **locally on your device** - Location data is stored in your browser's local storage - We do NOT have a central server that collects or stores user data ## 3. How We Use Your Information - To provide real-time location sharing between devices - To display phone number location based on country codes - To improve app functionality and user experience ## 4. Data Sharing - **No Central Server:** This app operates without a central server. All data remains on your device. - **Peer-to-Peer Sharing:** Location data is only shared when you explicitly share a link with another device. - **No Third-Party Sharing:** We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. ## 5. Permissions We Request ### Location Permission - Used to access your device's GPS for location sharing - You can revoke this permission at any time through your device settings ## 6. Children's Privacy - This app is not intended for children under 13 - We do not knowingly collect personal information from children ## 7. Security - Data is stored locally on your device using secure browser storage - Location sharing links contain encrypted device information - Users are responsible for sharing their location links securely ## 8. Changes to This Policy We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document. ## 9. Contact Us If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the app's support channels. --- **Note:** Phone number lookups show the country/city associated with a phone number's country code. This is not real-time GPS tracking and should not be used for emergency or illegal purposes.
